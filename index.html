About TGR Haas F1 Team

We don't come from a dynasty. We didn't inherit a trophy cabinet or decades of legacy. We built this from the ground up, and that's exactly what makes us different.

TGR Haas F1 Team is American at heart, global in reach, and completely uninterested in doing things the way they've always been done. Since arriving on the grid in 2016, we've operated on instinct, intelligence, and a refusal to accept that resources alone determine results.

We find solutions where others see limitations. We compete where others assume we can't. We are drivers, engineers, mechanics, strategists, people spread across two continents who show up every race weekend with one shared goal. Go faster than we did before, and make it count when it matters most.

This team is growing. The ambition is real. And we're just getting started. Join us on our journey to the front.
